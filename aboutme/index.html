About me

Kevin Bingham

I have a few things that I enjoy doing. I enjoy sports. If you can play it I enjoy it. I enjoy reading. My favorite genre is fantasy. If I had to choose a favorite book I would probably say Dawn of Wonder. I enjoy playing games, be they video, card or board games.I enjoy watching good wholesome shows and movies. I especially enjoy action and comedy.

I have been a member of the Church of Jesus Christ for my whole life. I served a mission in Sacramento California and loved it. I have a testimony of the Gospel of Jesus Christ, and the Savior.
